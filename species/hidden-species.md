# Hidden species

This page covers species that are concealed, misunderstood, or intentionally overlooked.

In a modern-fantasy setting, secrecy changes everything. Record who stays hidden, how they do it, and what happens when that secrecy fails.

## For each species

Capture:

* Why they stay hidden
* How they avoid exposure
* Who knows they exist
* What they risk by being seen

## Questions worth answering

* Do they hide by choice, by law, or by fear?
* Are they embedded in Mountain View or outside it?
* What magic protects them, exposes them, or puts them at risk?

{% hint style="info" %}
Hidden species become more compelling when their secrecy creates pressure on modern life.
{% endhint %}
