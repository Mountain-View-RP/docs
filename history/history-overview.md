# History overview

History gives the setting weight.

Use this section to record the past in a way that matters to the present. Every major era should leave something behind in Mountain View.

## Start here

* [Ancient history](ancient-history.md)
* [Modern history](modern-history.md)

## What belongs in history

* Founding myths, first records, and lost ages
* Wars, disasters, discoveries, and political shifts
* Events that still shape culture, fear, law, or power

{% hint style="info" %}
Tie each era to a visible consequence in the present day.
{% endhint %}
