# Districts and landmarks

This page breaks Mountain View into playable locations.

Use it to document neighborhoods, transit corridors, landmarks, hidden sites, and places shaped by magic or local history.

## For each district

Capture:

* Public identity
* Who holds power there
* What locals want or fear
* Why characters would go there

## For each landmark

Capture:

* What it is
* Why it matters
* Who claims it
* What secrets, risks, or opportunities it holds

{% hint style="info" %}
If a place matters to more than one page, link it from history, species, and magic as well.
{% endhint %}
